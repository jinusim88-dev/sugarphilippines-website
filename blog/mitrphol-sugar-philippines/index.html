Mitrphol Sugar Philippines: Buyer's Guide to Thai Refined Sugar

Everything you need to know about Mitrphol — the world's 4th largest sugar brand, now available wholesale in the Philippines.

Published: March 15, 2026 5 min read By: SugarPhilippines Team

If you're searching for Mitrphol sugar in the Philippines, you're looking at one of the most trusted refined sugar brands in Asia. Whether you run a bakery, manufacture beverages, or produce confectionery for export, here's what you need to know about sourcing Mitrphol wholesale.

What Is Mitrphol Sugar?

Mitrphol (also written as Mitr Phol) is produced by Mitr Phol Group, the largest sugar producer in Thailand and Asia. As of 2024, Mitr Phol ranks as the world's 4th largest sugar producer, operating seven sugar mills across Thailand with a combined processing capacity of over 130,000 tonnes of sugarcane per day.

The company was founded in 1946 as a small family business in Ratchaburi province, producing condensed syrup. By 1956 they began producing their own sugar, and have since expanded into a global operation spanning Thailand, China, Laos, and Australia. Their annual sugar output is approximately two million tonnes.

For Philippine buyers, Mitrphol represents an imported alternative to locally milled sugar — offering ICUMSA 45 purity, which is the highest commercially available grade of refined white sugar.

Why Philippine Buyers Choose Mitrphol

Mitrphol has gained traction among Philippine food manufacturers and commercial buyers for several reasons:

  • ICUMSA 45 purity — the international gold standard for refined sugar. Polarization of =99.80% with a color rating of =45 I.U., meaning ultra-white, ultra-pure sugar.
  • Consistent quality — as the world's 4th largest producer, Mitr Phol has industrial-scale quality control that delivers batch-to-batch consistency.
  • International certifications — Halal certified, Non-GMO, and Allergen-free. Important for manufacturers producing export-grade products.
  • Fast dissolving — the fine granulation and high purity make Mitrphol ideal for beverages, syrups, and confectionery where clarity matters.
Key Takeaway

Mitrphol is the right choice if your products need the highest purity refined sugar available — especially for export-quality confectionery, clear beverages, pharmaceutical-grade applications, and any product where ICUMSA 45 is specified.

Who Should Buy Mitrphol in the Philippines?

Confectionery & Chocolate Producers

If you manufacture candies, chocolates, or premium desserts — especially for export — Mitrphol's ultra-low color and high purity give you a cleaner end product that meets international buyer specs.

Beverage Manufacturers

For bottled drinks, juice concentrates, syrups, and milk tea operations, ICUMSA 45 dissolves completely without any cloudiness or residual color. This is critical for clear or light-colored beverages.

Pharmaceutical & Nutraceutical Companies

Companies producing pharmaceutical-grade syrups, supplements, or health products often require ICUMSA 45 as a baseline specification. Mitrphol's Halal and Non-GMO certifications add further compliance value.

Export-Oriented Food Manufacturers

If your products are exported to markets that specify ICUMSA 45 sugar as an ingredient standard, Mitrphol gives you a documented, internationally recognized source.
